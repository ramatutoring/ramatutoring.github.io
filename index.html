About Us

Ms. Rama, the founder of this tutoring center holds a Bachelor's and a Master's degree in Physics from Osmania University, India. She has taught Math, Physics, and Chemistry to undergraduate students for 20+ years. In the past few years, she has been tutoring SAT math, AP Physics, honors Physics, and precalculus to students. She has also taught math and science to elementary and junior high students. Rama has experience coaching highly talented students towards Physics olympiads as well as teaching special needs students. She is passionate about STEM, especially helping students get rid of their fear of math and enjoys working with all types of students.

Why Rama's Tutoring

  1. Rama understands that Physics and Math can be daunting to students who end up hating the subject, leading to their poor performance in classes. Her expertise lies is in teaching difficult material in such a way that students can quickly understand concepts and retain them longer.
  2. Given her years of teaching experience, she is skilled in customizing her teaching methods to suit the individual's learning ability, and focuses on ensuring enough practice for the student.
  3. Rama has lectured in classes with 100+ students, taught in small and large groups, and tutored students one-on-one. Regardless of the medium, she builds rapport with students and her teaching style ensures equal attention to all students.
